ü•ë Avocado Toast

Type: Vegetarian, High-Protein

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh.
  3. Smash the avocado with a fork and season with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado onto the toasted bread.
  5. Top with red pepper flakes, if desired. Serve immediately.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  2. Place chicken wings in a bowl and toss with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.

ü•í Cucumber Slices with Hummus

Type: Vegetarian, Low-Carb

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Slice the cucumber into thin rounds.
  2. Arrange the slices on a plate and top with a spoonful of hummus.
  3. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top, if desired.
  4. Serve chilled as a refreshing snack.

üçì Fruit Salad

Type: Vegetarian

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the chopped fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey and lemon juice and toss gently.
  3. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.

üçñ Beef Jerky

Type: Non-Vegetarian

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean beef, sliced thinly
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 170¬∞F (77¬∞C).
  2. Mix soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, black pepper, and honey in a bowl.
  3. Marinate the beef slices in the mixture for 4 hours or overnight in the fridge.
  4. Place the beef slices on a baking rack and bake for 4-6 hours, flipping occasionally, until fully dried.
  5. Let it cool before serving.
