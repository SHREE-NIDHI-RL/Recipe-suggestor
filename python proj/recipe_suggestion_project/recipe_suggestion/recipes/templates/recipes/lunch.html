Type: Vegetarian

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes

üçó Grilled Chicken Salad

Type: Non-Vegetarian, High-Protein

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked and slice it.
  2. In a bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and chicken.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, toss, and serve fresh.

ü•© Beef Lettuce Wraps

Type: Non-Vegetarian, High-Protein, Low-Carb

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Butter lettuce leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet until browned.
  2. Add soy sauce, garlic powder, and chili flakes.
  3. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until well-seasoned.
  4. Serve spoonfuls of beef in lettuce leaves as wraps.

  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until translucent.
